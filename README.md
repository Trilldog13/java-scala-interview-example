# Senior Backend (Scala/Java) Engineer (Picking & Orchestration) Interview Framework Example

This week I’ve been focusing on the **Senior Backend Software Engineer (Picking & Orchestration)** role.

This role is central to our In-Store Fulfilment (ISF) mission, where we build cloud-based systems to automate online customer orders at scale across 11 countries. Candidates should understand that we are looking for strong backend engineers who can bridge the gap between core JVM engineering and the future of AI-assisted development.This framework helps candidates prepare by outlining our focus on distributed systems, high availability, and collaborative architectural design

> **Note:** This repository is a process guide. It contains no technical answers or solutions to our tasks; it is designed to give you a flavour of what to expect during your journey with us.

## 🎯 Our Mission
Our In-Store Fulfilment (ISF) department designs and develops distributed cloud-based systems and web technologies to automate online customer orders at scale. We operate in over 1,000 stores across 4 continents, handling more than 300,000 weekly orders

## 🛠 The Interview Stages

### 🟢 Stage 1: Hiring Manager Screen (30 Mins)
**Intent:** Alignment on role expectations and technical background.
**Evaluation:** Discussion of your experience with high-availability systems
**AI Mindset:** We value hands-on experience or a strong interest in developing AI-assisted practices

### 🔵 Stage 2: Technical Deep Dive (120 Mins)
A two-part session focusing on architectural breadth and depth.

#### Part A: Software Architecture & Code Review (40 Mins)
*No live coding is required.*
- **Assessment Areas:**
    * Clean code and Object-Oriented Design.
    * Concurrency at the code level and Data Structures.
    * Security, Error Handling, and Unit Testing.

#### Part B: System Architecture (40 Mins)
*Focus: Designing a scalable distributed system (e.g., URL shortener).*
- **Assessment Areas:**
    * Scalability, High Availability, and Caching.
    * SQL vs. NoSQL and transaction consistency.
    * Networking and REST web services.

### 🟠 Stage 3: Paired Programming (120 Mins)
**Intent:** Assessing hands-on engineering and real-world collaboration.
**Evaluation:** Application of JVM languages (Java/Scala) to solve complex domain problems
**The Goal:** Demonstrate a product- and customer-focused mindset where technology is a tool to deliver value

## ⚖️ Evaluation & Fairness
We ensure a level playing field by focusing on:
**Structured Scoring:** Candidates are evaluated on system resilience patterns and best practices
**AI Integration:** We champion embedding AI tools like Claude Code, Gemini, and GitHub Copilot into daily workflows
**Leadership:** We value the ability to lead technical initiatives and articulate decisions to stakeholders

## 🔒 Privacy & Data Handling
* **Purpose:** All information collected during the interview process is used solely to evaluate your candidacy for this specific role.
* **Security:** Your data is handled in compliance with global privacy standards and local data protection laws.
* **Candidate Respect:** We value your time and privacy; our goal is a professional, supportive, and secure hiring experience.
